The K1R...B Awards a tribute to innovations for a better life. The Prize highlights the extensive impacts of crypto currecy and Blockchain innovation on society, even on humanity at large. General principles of the K1by Krypto Prize:

  • Awarded to disruptive crypto economic and technological innovations enhancing the quality of systems, people’s and DAO's lives in a sustainable manner.
  • Crypto economic innovations applied in practice and delivering extensive change now on and into the future.
  • Crypto economic innovations stimulating further cutting edge research and development in science and technology, with P2P Blockchain Solutions.

The K1by Krypto Prize encourages further cutting-edge research and development and highlights the internet's practical and solution-focused mindset – one of the greatest strengths of the deep web – for a better world.

Candidates for the K1by Krypto Prize are accepted from across the internet and all fields of technology and reserach excluding banking and institutional non P2P technology. In a global world, people’s lives are affected by common problems and solutions. It is therefore also essential that a K1by Krypto prize can be awarded to anyone and anywhere in the world.
